Formulas and Assumptions

Formulas:

After tax income = gross annual earned income - federal tax - state tax
Target spending = (50% * after-tax income) + (5% * current asset base)
Target saving = after tax income - target spending
Worker target(savings) = 10% * gross annual income
Worker target(spending) = after tax income - worker target(savings)


Assumptions:

You have a fixed annual salary with standard deductions for federal tax.
7% annual returns on savings invested in the market.
2025 federal tax brackets are the tax brackets that will be used to project 15 years in the future.
The capital gains rate is 20%.
If the worker's savings target exceeds the investor's target, then the higher worker target becomes the new investor target.
If the worker's spending target is lower than the investor's target, then the lower worker target becomes the new investor target.
